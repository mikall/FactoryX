# Step 4: Context Extraction

> **Previous state**: Process tier selected and documented (Step 3).

## Goal

Extract structured business context from intake materials.

## Agent: `product-manager`

From the ingested materials, extract:

1. **Business domain** and market positioning
2. **Target users** and their jobs-to-be-done
3. **Value proposition** — what problem we solve and why it matters
4. **Success metrics** — how we'll know it's working
5. **Competitive landscape** — what exists, where we differentiate
6. **Constraints** — budget, timeline, team size, regulatory
7. **MVP scope** — what must be in v1 vs what can wait

## Agent: `ux-designer` (if activated for this project type)

From intake materials with UI/UX relevance, extract:

1. **User personas** — who are the primary and secondary users
2. **Key user journeys** — the critical paths users will follow
3. **Usability constraints** — accessibility needs, device targets, user expertise level
4. **UX references** — competitor UIs, wireframes, mockups found in intake

## Output

Structured business context brief — will feed the Business Context section of `project.md`.
