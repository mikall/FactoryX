- **Before sprint planning**: read the latest entries to avoid repeating mistakes and replicate what worked

- **After bug fix**: add entries when the root cause reveals a systemic pattern (Phase 5 of `/quick-fix`)
- **Keep it actionable**: every entry must have a concrete takeaway, not just a description
